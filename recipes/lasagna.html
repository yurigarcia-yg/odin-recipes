Lasagna

Learn to make lasagna

The following two lists provide the ingredients and steps to make what's going to be your favorite dish!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
